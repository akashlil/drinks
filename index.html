Wanderlust Spirits was founded with exploration and adventure in mind. We travel the world and source each ingredient by hand. Blending those ingredients with Icelandic water, we created a unique flavor experience for our wandering family of spirits. Our first creation was Helix7 Vodka. Our spirit is sourced from the wheat fields of Champagne France, and then travels to Iceland. There, it is blended, and carbon filtered for purity.
